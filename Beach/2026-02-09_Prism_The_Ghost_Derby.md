## The Insight
Sports simulations like *NBA 2K* aren't popular just because of the physics engine. They are popular because they allow us to resolve **inter-generational arguments**.
-   "Could '96 Jordan beat '16 Curry?"
-   "Could '73 Secretariat beat '22 Flightline?"

We aren't just simulating a race. We are simulating a **Ghost Derby**.

## The Mechanism: The "Soul" Stat
In *NBA 2K*, players have "Badges" (Clutch Shooter, Intimidator). Horses need the same narrative-layer stats, not just speed/stamina.
-   **The Closer:** Speed multiplier in the final 200m if behind. (e.g., Deep Impact).
-   **The Frontrunner:** Debuff to stamina if not leading. (e.g., Silence Suzuka).
-   **The Mudder:** Stat boost on heavy tracks.

## The Product Vision
Don't build a "Betting Simulator." Build a **"Valhalla Engine."**
-   **Mode:** "Dream Match." Select any 18 horses from history.
-   **Visual:** Low-poly or stylized is fine. The *drama* is in the commentary and the camera angles, not the hair physics.
-   **Loop:** Collect the "Souls" (Data Cards) -> Train them (Modern Regimen) -> Race them (The Derby).
